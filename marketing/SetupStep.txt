# Deployment and DNS Setup Guide for Bigweb Reimagined

This guide outlines the steps to deploy the project to Vercel, configure the necessary environment variables, and set up your custom domain (bigwebdigital.com).

## Phase 1: Preparation
1. Ensure your latest code is pushed to your Git repository (GitHub, GitLab, or Bitbucket).
2. Have your production environment variables ready (from your local `.env` file).
3. Have the login credentials for your domain registrar (where you purchased bigwebdigital.com) ready.

## Phase 2: Adding the Project to Vercel
1. Log in to your Vercel account at [https://vercel.com](https://vercel.com).
2. From your Vercel dashboard, click the **"Add New..."** button in the top right and select **"Project"**.
3. In the "Import Git Repository" section, locate your Bigweb Reimagined repository and click **"Import"**.
4. **Configure Project:**
   - **Project Name:** You can leave it as the repository name or change it (e.g., `bigweb-reimagined`).
   - **Framework Preset:** Vercel should automatically detect the framework you are using (e.g., Next.js or Vite). Confirm it is correct.
   - **Root Directory:** Leave this as `./` unless your main application code is nested in a subdirectory.

## Phase 3: Setting Up Environment Variables
*Before you click Deploy, you need to add your environment variables so the build process and application work correctly.*

1. On the same "Configure Project" page, expand the **"Environment Variables"** section.
2. Open your local `.env` file.
3. For each variable (e.g., your Supabase URLs and Keys), copy the Key and the Value.
4. Paste them into the Vercel Environment Variables inputs and click **"Add"**.
   - *Tip: If you have a `.env` file, you can often copy the entire contents of the file and paste it into the first "Key" input on Vercel, and it will automatically parse and add all of them.*
5. Once all necessary variables are added, click the **"Deploy"** button.
6. Wait for Vercel to build and deploy your project. Once it says "Congratulations!", your app is live on a `.vercel.app` subdomain.

## Phase 4: Setting Up DNS for bigwebdigital.com
*Now that the site is live, let's connect your custom domain.*

1. Click **"Continue to Dashboard"** on the Vercel deployment success screen.
2. Navigate to the **"Settings"** tab for your project.
3. In the left sidebar, click on **"Domains"**.
4. In the input field, type `bigwebdigital.com` and click **"Add"**.
5. Vercel will ask how you want to handle the `www` subdomain. It is highly recommended to select the option to add `www.bigwebdigital.com` and redirect it to `bigwebdigital.com` (or vice versa, depending on your preference).
6. **Configure DNS Records:** Vercel will now show you the DNS records you need to configure. You generally have two choices:

   **Option A: Vercel Nameservers (Recommended for easiest management)**
   - Log in to your domain registrar (e.g., GoDaddy, Namecheap).
   - Find the "Nameservers" or "DNS Management" section for `bigwebdigital.com`.
   - Change your nameservers to "Custom" and enter the ones Vercel provides:
     - `ns1.vercel-dns.com`
     - `ns2.vercel-dns.com`
   - Save the changes. Note: This removes DNS management from your registrar and moves it to Vercel.

   **Option B: A Record & CNAME (Use this if you use custom email like Google Workspace on this domain)**
   - Keep your current nameservers at your registrar.
   - Go to your registrar's DNS Records page.
   - Add/Update the **A Record** for the root domain:
     - Type: `A`
     - Name/Host: `@` (or leave blank)
     - Value: `76.76.21.21`
   - Add/Update the **CNAME Record** for the www subdomain:
     - Type: `CNAME`
     - Name/Host: `www`
     - Value: `cname.vercel-dns.com`

7. Return to the Vercel Domains settings page. Vercel will actively check the DNS propagation.
8. Once the DNS changes propagate (which can take a few minutes to a few hours), Vercel will show a valid configuration and automatically provision a free SSL certificate. 

Your website is now successfully deployed and accessible at `https://bigwebdigital.com`!
